Thanks for visiting the site! I solve a wide range of data puzzles as a consultant at Third Sector Intelligence (3SI) in Seattle.

I use whatever tools are best suited to the analysis and data management work. Typically, I seek solutions in R, Python, and SQL, but develop products in whatever software and code is necessary to get the job done well. Clients tend to like Tableau these days, so I use it too.

Before joining 3SI, I completed an MPA at the University of Washington Evans School of Governance and Public Policy and an MA at the United Nations University for Peace. I also lived in Latin America for many years, where I taught in the IB Diploma program at United World College, edited a peace and conflict journal, and developed strategy for implementing governance and social mobility projects.

You can find me on LinkedIn, GitHub, and on the 3SI staff site.

If we meet, ask me about: the best podcast episode ever, stingless bees, plantintas, light roast coffees (because they're better), and where to eat in Seattle.
